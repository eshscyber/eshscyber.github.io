CyberPatriot

CyberPatriot is a computer security-based competition which teaches students how to defend and protect computers. Students compete by resloving issues in virtual copies of Windows and Linux as well as by using siumation programs to work on network security. The finalists of the competition receive an expense-paid trip to Washington D.C. to compete nationally!
